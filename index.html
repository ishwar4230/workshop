G+4 Commercial Building
Design Workshop

MIDAS R&D CENTRE

“It’s not the beauty of a building you should look at; it's the construction of the foundation that will stand the test of time” - David Allan Coe




Megalith in collaboration with MIDAS R&D CENTRE is set to organize the workshops which will present an opportunity to learn from the professionals with years of experience in technology and management of Building Design. This gives MIDAS the right pedigree to provide work specific comprehensive consultancy and quality education. This workshop on commercial building design will use MIDAS Gen software and will aim to familiarize participants of exhaustive analysis, design, and drafting of a solution for a variety of building and general structures.





To know more about "MIDAS Gen " software,
Click Here!


Benefits

  • The major objective of the workshop is to practically train participants about the design and analysis of complex and large structures.
  • Certificate of participation from Midas R&D Centre & Megalith, IIT Kharagpur


Duration

The workshop will be conducted by MIDAS R&D CENTRE on 9th March 2022. A one day workshop comprising of 2 sessions of 3 hours to be provided on MIDAS Gen to the participants with a hands-on session on theory as well as practical problems.

How to register

Registration Procedure

  • Sign up as participant for Megalith 2022. (*skip this step if register already)
  • Sign in using your registered credentials to access the "My Profile " portal.
  • In the "My Profile " portal, go to "Workshop Details " section. Click on the "Register Now " button to register for the workshop.
  • You need to pay the required workshop fees to complete the workshop registration process at the registration desk of Megalith 2022.
  • The workshop fees does not include the accommodation and events fee of the fest. You will have to pay separately for accommodation/events at the registration desk.
  • The accommodation fee for workshop participants will be charged on day to day basis.





Workshop Fee Details

  • Workshop 1 (MIDAS Gen): ₹ 500
  • Workshop 2 (Tekla Structures): ₹ 500
  • Combination of Workshop 1 and 2: Charges ₹ 800 (You will save ₹ 200 for taking both workshops)
  • Payment mode: Offline (Pay at the registration desk of Megalith 2022)



Workshop Dates

  • Workshop 1 (MIDAS Gen) : March 9th, 2022.
  • Workshop 2 (Tekla Structures) : March 10th, 2022

Note :All workshop participants are requested to bring their own laptops.
